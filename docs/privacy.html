InkSlate Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 2025

InkSlate ("we," "our," or "the app") is committed to protecting your privacy. This policy describes how we handle your data.

Data Collection

InkSlate stores your data locally on your device. We collect and process:

  • Notes, journals, todos, recipes, places, and other content you create
  • Photos you attach (with your permission)
  • Calendar data (only if you grant calendar access)
  • App preferences such as folder selection and menu order

Data Storage & Sync

  • Local storage: Your data is stored on your device using Core Data
  • iCloud: Optional sync across Apple devices via CloudKit using your Apple ID
  • We do not store your data on our own servers

Third-Party Services

The Movie Database (TMDB): Used for Watchlist. TMDB Privacy Policy

Apple Services: Calendar, Photos, iCloud—Apple Privacy Policy

Data Sharing

We do not sell or share your personal data. Your data stays on your device and in your iCloud account.

Data Deletion

Use Factory Reset in Settings → Danger Zone to permanently erase all app data. Deleting the app removes local data.

Contact

For questions, open an issue on GitHub.
