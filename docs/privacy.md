# InkSlate Privacy Policy

**Last updated:** March 2025

InkSlate ("we," "our," or "the app") is committed to protecting your privacy. This policy describes how we handle your data.

## Data Collection

InkSlate stores your data locally on your device. We collect and process:

- **Notes, journals, todos, recipes, places, and other content** you create in the app
- **Photos** you attach to notes, recipes, places, or your profile (with your permission)
- **Calendar data** (only if you grant calendar access)
- **App preferences** such as folder selection and menu order

## Data Storage & Sync

- **Local storage:** Your data is stored on your device using Core Data
- **iCloud:** If you sign in with your Apple ID, InkSlate can sync your data across your Apple devices via CloudKit. Sync is optional and uses your personal iCloud account
- **We do not** store your data on our own servers

## Third-Party Services

- **The Movie Database (TMDB):** Used for movie and TV show information in the Watchlist feature. TMDB has its own [privacy policy](https://www.themoviedb.org/privacy-policy)
- **Apple Services:** Calendar, Photos, iCloud—governed by [Apple's Privacy Policy](https://www.apple.com/legal/privacy/)

## Data Sharing

We do not sell or share your personal data with third parties for advertising or marketing. Your data stays on your device and in your iCloud account (if you use sync).

## Data Deletion

You can delete all your data at any time:
- Use **Factory Reset** in Settings → Danger Zone to permanently erase all app data
- Deleting the app removes local data from your device
- iCloud data can be managed in your Apple ID settings

## Security

- Optional password/biometric protection is available for encrypted notes
- Data transmitted to iCloud is encrypted by Apple
- We do not transmit your content to our own servers

## Children's Privacy

InkSlate is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect data from children.

## Changes

We may update this policy from time to time. The "Last updated" date at the top indicates when changes were last made. Continued use of the app after changes constitutes acceptance.

## Contact

For questions about this privacy policy, please open an issue on the [InkSlate GitHub repository](https://github.com/sirlwaldron/InkSlate).
